Scrum Tools

I've created the following bookmarklets to aid scrum teams. Drag into your bookmark bar.

Standup Clock
  • Unobtrusive countdown
  • Defaults to 15 min
  • Configurable starting minutes
  • Quick snap to next quarter hour
  • Not annoying when elapsed
F10 - Add Points
  • Requires Scrum for Trello Chome Extension
  • Once activated, F10 adds the hovered card points to total
  • Unobtrusive with Trello
F9 - Points Above
  • Requires Scrum for Trello Chome Extension
  • Once activated, F9 sums card points above hovered card
  • Supports filters
  • Unobtrusive with Trello
